Services Offered

Sales

Parisukat Square Realty sells condominium units in the top locations of Metro Manila, Philippines. In partnership with DMCI Homes, Parisukat Square Realty is able to build communities where families create a place of their own and can truly call home. These residential communities offer the best in modern in-city living and are located in close proximity to major business and commercial centers of Makati, Ortigas, and the Bonifacio Global City. At the end of the day, when you want to just relax and get away from it all, these communities are equipped with complete resort amenities such as clubhouses, swimming pools, and parks. More importantly, properties are safe, secure, and enjoyable investments for a bright future; for you and your family. Visit our Project Portfolio and Contact Us TODAY!
