HOW TO GET THERE

Via Commute

From Manila, take a bus going to Cabanatuan via AH26 Pan-Philippine Hwy route. Take off at Jollibee in AH26 corner Circumferencial Road. Ride a tricycle going to Lumina Cabanatuan City site.

Via Private Vehicle

From Manila, take NLEX, then SCTEX. Exit at Tarlac City, then take a right turn to Cabanatuan City. Take another left turn at Santa Rosa crossing, then right at Jollibee in AH26 corner Circumferencial Road. Turn right before NE Bodega then turn left on Emilio Vergara Hwy. Drive straight and you will find the Lumina marker.
