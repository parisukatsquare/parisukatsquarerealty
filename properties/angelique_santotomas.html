About this Property

Barangay San Vicente, Sto. Tomas, Batangas

About 45 minutes from Alabang, Sto. Tomas, Batangas can be easily reached via STAR Tollway and SLEX. Cushioned between Sto. Tomas and Tanauan is Lumina’s flagship project, Lumina Batangas. Graced with Mt. Makiling’s majestic view, the33-hectare Lumina Batangas is complete with a guard house, multi-purpose hall, mini gardens and playground, basketball court, and space for future commercial establishments.

HOW TO GET THERE

Via Commute

From Manila, take a bus going to Batangas via Tanauan-Lipa route. Take off at Tanauan Water District. Ride a tricycle going to Lumina Batangas site.

Via Private Vehicle

From Manila, take SLEX, then STAR Tollway. Exit at Sto. Tomas, then take a left turn to Tanauan. Take another left turn at Tanauan Water District, then North Elementary School. Turn right to Camella Frontierra to go to Lumina Batangas site.
